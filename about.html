Our Story

How Coast BC Painting
Was Built

Coast BC Painting was built on a simple belief — painting should be done properly, not rushed.

The journey began in 2020, when the founder started working under an experienced painting contractor in the Lower Mainland. During those early years, he gained hands-on experience working on homes and commercial projects across Surrey and surrounding cities.

While working in the industry, he noticed something many homeowners experience — some contractors cut corners to finish jobs faster or save on materials. He believed that if more time, care, and effort were put into the preparation, products, and finishing work, the results would last longer and look significantly better.

That belief led him to start Coast BC Painting.

Today, the goal is simple: deliver clean, professional painting work where the quality speaks for itself. Every project is approached with attention to detail, proper preparation, and respect for the customer’s space.

That commitment to quality is why every job is backed by a 5-year touch-up guarantee.

Our Approach

How We Approach Every Job

The same care and attention goes into every project, no matter the size.

Proper surface preparation

We sand, clean, prime, and prepare every surface before a drop of paint goes on. Prep work is what makes paint last.

Clean and respectful work environment

We protect your floors, furniture, and landscaping. We leave every space cleaner than we found it.

Attention to detail from start to finish

Clean edges, smooth finishes, no roller marks. We take our time and don't rush the final result.

Honest pricing and clear communication

You get a straightforward quote with no hidden costs. We communicate clearly before, during, and after the job.

Work backed by a 5-year touch-up guarantee

1 free touch-up within 5 years of job completion. We stand behind the quality of our work.
